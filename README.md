# Beverage Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes beverage sales performance across regions, brands, retailers, and time using Power BI.  
The goal is to identify key revenue drivers, evaluate profitability, and understand seasonal demand patterns.

## Dashboard Preview
[Dashboard.png](Dashboard.png)

## Business Questions
This dashboard answers several key business questions:

- Which regions generate the highest revenue?
- Which beverage brands drive the most profit?
- Which retailers contribute the most sales?
- How does sales performance change throughout the year?

## Key Metrics
The dashboard uses several financial performance indicators:

| Metric | Definition |
|------|------|
| Revenue | Sum of total sales |
| Operating Profit | Sum of operating profit |
| Operating Margin | Profit ÷ Revenue |
| Units Sold | Total product volume |
| Average Price | Revenue ÷ Units Sold |

## Dashboard Sections

### Executive KPIs
High-level business performance indicators including revenue, profit, margin, units sold, and average price.

### Regional Performance
Revenue comparison across different regions to identify strong and weak markets.

### Brand Performance
Analysis of product portfolio performance based on units sold, pricing, and profit contribution.

### Retailer Performance
Evaluation of sales and profitability by distribution channel.

### Monthly Sales Trend
Time-series analysis to detect seasonality and demand patterns.

## Key Insights

### 1. Regional Market Performance
The West region generates the highest revenue among all regions, driven by strong demand in large metropolitan cities.

### 2. Brand Leadership
Coca-Cola is the leading brand in both revenue and operating profit, indicating strong market dominance and high sales volume.

### 3. Seasonal Demand Patterns
Sales peak during summer months, suggesting strong seasonal demand for beverage products.

### 4. Retail Channel Strategy
Sodapop is the largest revenue-generating retailer, highlighting its importance as a key distribution partner.

## Business Recommendations

• Increase marketing campaigns in underperforming regions such as the Midwest  
• Strengthen partnerships with high-performing retailers  
• Expand production capacity ahead of summer demand peaks
